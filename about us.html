Our Machinery

By employing cutting-edge cutting, polishing, and lifting machinery, we maintain the highest standards of quality and efficiency in our granite production processes. Our advanced equipment enables us to deliver precisely cut, beautifully polished, and safely handled granite products to our valued customers.

Cutting Machinery

At our granite industry, we utilize state-of-the-art cutting machinery to ensure precision and efficiency in shaping granite stones. To bring intricate designs and shapes to life on granite surfaces, we employ CNC routers. With CNC technology, we can achieve precise and complex cuts according to our clients' specifications.

Polishing Machinery

To enhance the natural beauty of granite and achieve a smooth, glossy finish, we employ cutting-edge polishing machinery. Through multiple polishing stages using various grit sizes of diamond abrasives, we achieve a flawless, high-gloss finish. Our edge profiling machines ensure smooth and perfectly finished edges for countertops, vanity tops, and other granite applications.

Lifting Machinery

To ensure the safe handling and transportation of heavy granite slabs and blocks, we employ reliable lifting machinery. With the help of lifting devices such as hooks or clamps, we ensure secure and efficient lifting and movement of granite within our facility.
